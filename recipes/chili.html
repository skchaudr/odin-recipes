Chili Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 2 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Add the chopped onion, bell pepper, and garlic to the pot. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  3. Stir in the diced tomatoes, kidney beans, and tomato sauce.
  4. Add the chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.
  5. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for at least 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
